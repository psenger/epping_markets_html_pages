The Epping Twilight Markets is Our Lady Help of Christian’s Primary School’s (OLHC) annual fundraising event. All funds raised goes towards school resources for the school children. It has evolved into a fun-filled event for the greater Epping and surrounding communities.

The Epping Twilight Markets offers an abundant choice of premium, local-sourced produce, gourmet foods, gifts and so much more. Situated centrally in Sydney’s northern districts, our stallholders are set throughout the OLHC school grounds (behind Our Lady Help of Christians Catholic Church). Entry can be made via Cambridge or Oxford Streets, Epping.

Come and enjoy something to eat, some shopping and an entertaining night out in our welcoming local community.

Tickets for the Major Raffle will be on sale at the Market between 5pm-7pm prior to the draw.
